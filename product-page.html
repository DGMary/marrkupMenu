Hi! We glad introduce you 3d model - STL files - to print your own Plasma Pistol H2A from world well known video game Halo.

Lot include:
- 58 stl files to 3d printing (biggest part demensions: 182*114*42 mm).
- 3 stl files - stand for Plasma Pistol.
- Digital Instruction for assembling.
- List of components you will needs for assembling with discription.

List of components needs for assembling with LEDs lighting:
- 3-4mm LEDs
- 22ohm resistors
- 1xAAA batteries slot
- 8*8mm button switch
- M3 screws
- wires
- 3pcs. round magnets 10*5mm and 1pcs. round magnets 8*2mm

The model assumes a demo stand, removable parts and the presence of LED backlight.

Plasma Pistol dimensions:
338.7mm x 272.8mm x 46mm
